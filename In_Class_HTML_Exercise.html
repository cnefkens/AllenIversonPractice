How To Make A PP&J Sandwich

  1. Open bread!
  2. take out two pieces of bread
  3. get peanut butter and jelly out of cupboard
  4. take out knife from drawer
  5. open up peanut butter and use knife to spread it onto one of the pieces of bread
  6. open up jelly and use knife to spread onto the other piece of bread
  7. put pieces of bread together and use knife to cut in half
  8. enjoy
